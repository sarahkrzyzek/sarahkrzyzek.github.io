Nice to meet you!

Let me tell you a bit about myself...

graphic design

I've got seven years of experience designing online and print communications, working with high-profile brands, developing and implementing marketing campaigns and I always strive to deliver compelling and on-brand materials that will fit your needs and goals.

photography

Photography is a great passion of mine and my Nikon and I are up for any challenge. This interest has further developed into three years of social media management with Instagram and Facebook.

web design

I've got two years of creatively leading UX/UI experiences, from creating sketches, to wireframing prototypes, to user testing, to designing final pixel-perfect mockups in Photoshop. I also have an understanding of CSS3 and HTML5.

illustration

I am an experienced watercolor, gouache, and acrylic painter, as well as a strong digital illustrator.
